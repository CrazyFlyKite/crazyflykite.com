Requirements

Rate Requirements

Base Requirements

  • If your level has very good gameplay, but the deco isn't the best, it can still be rated higher. For example: a level with Feature worthy deco, but it's rated Epic because it has great gameplay.
  • Longer levels get higher ratings, for example: "Our Time" by boxie.
  • Creativity will give higher ratings. For example: "Last Life" by ProfWhizz. The background is pretty empty and the structuring is pretty odd, but it's very creative and well-made.
  • Levels have to be at least 15 seconds.
  • Levels must be verified.
  • Levels should be consistent. Don't have one part be a 6 star but the next is an Extreme Demon. The same for deco: one part shouldn't be Feature and another is Mythic.
  • If a level's gameplay is considered very bad by a large majority of players, the level won't be rated even if the deco is rate worthy.
  • Levels must not be harder than approximately Top 400 on AREDL.

Each Rate Specific

  • Star Rate - The level needs to have some structuring. You can use simple 1.0 blocks or just solid blocks, the gameplay should be okay at least.
  • Feature - At least 30 seconds long. Your level needs to have decent deco and good gameplay, otherwise great gameplay with relatively simple deco. For example: "Teresa and Maria" by CrazyFlyKite.
  • Epic - At least 30 seconds long. Your level should have good deco with great gameplay. For example: "adrenaline" by SeaCarrrot.
  • Legendary - At least 30 seconds long. Your level should have excellent gameplay with excellent deco, and just overall a great level.
  • Mythic - At least 45 seconds long. Everyone should agree it's a wonderful level. Or it's a stupid joke level :3

Challenge List

Quality Standards

  • No bear-bones. At least structure the level.
  • No copying levels from vanilla GD and importing them here. If it's your level, it's fine.
  • No buffing levels and saying it's your own. Please, be truthful.
  • If you do buff the level, please redeco.

Shitty List

Quality Standards

  • Levels must be named "Shitty Level Name" (caps don't have to match).
  • It must be a layout style redeco of an already existing SeaGDPS level.
  • It has to be nerfed (meaning easier than the original level).
  • People can make different versions of the same level.
  • Your level can have effects and stuff, but it still has to be 1.0 blocks and stuff.
