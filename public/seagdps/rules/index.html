Demonlist Rules

General

  • You don't need a verification video for Hard Demons and below.
  • If you're hacking you'll be timed out, leaderboard banned and your level will be removed.

Harder Levels

  • If a level is harder, you have to provide a verification video to proof you beat the level (With clicks or taps, depending on the device, and no, you can't use the Click Sounds mod, you have to have clicks or taps).
  • Cheat indicator, clock and CPS are required for verification videos.
  • At least a second of the previous attempt should be shown (Unless the level was beaten on the first attempt).
  • The whole level complete screen should be shown.
